About Tommy

Tommy Attwood is a 21 year old artist from Los Angeles. He currently goes to college in New York City.

Tommy finds reality to be absurd, scary, and hilarious. His drawings are products of his thoughts and observations. Sometimes they're wholesome and uplifting; other times they're dark and nihilistic. This feels appropriate.
